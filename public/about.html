Welcome to AAEJEY Consumer Company

AAEJEY Consumer Company is a well-known FMCG manufacturer in Sri Lanka. With over 25 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. Our manufacturing facility in Polgahawela enables us to produce a wide range of FMCG products including liquid blue, air fresheners, naphthalene balls, ball point pens, liquid dish wash, laundry detergent, and toilet cleaner.

We take pride in our famous brand Nilma and other popular brands like Flowery, Mammoth, Rytink, Luty, Lovendry, and Mr. Doust. Explore our products and experience the quality and reliability that we offer.
